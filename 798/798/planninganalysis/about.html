Website Goal

The purpose of the website is an informational hub for my coding projects.

I want the website to consolidate all my coding projects into one coherent place for further convenience.

My intended audience is anyone who might be interested in my projects and who could possibly use some of my resources for help with their own projects.

The content of my site will be various explanations of my projects and a place to see all my projects.
